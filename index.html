A little tool, built with love

I'm a dad who built BeadSnap on weekend afternoons so my kids and I could turn our favorite photos into bead crafts at the kitchen table. No ads, no signups, no nonsense — just a tiny tool that hopefully sparks a few rainy-day art sessions in your home too.

🔒 Your photos never leave your browser — everything runs locally for your privacy.
