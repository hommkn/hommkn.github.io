About BeadSnap

I'm Chen, a dad and maker. I built BeadSnap because I wanted to turn my kids' photos and drawings into Perler bead patterns without paying for bloated software or signing up for accounts. BeadSnap runs entirely in your browser — your photos never touch a server. It's free, forever.

Bead crafting is one of our family's favorite offline activities. There's something calming and deeply satisfying about placing tiny colored beads one by one — it pulls kids (and adults) away from screens and into a creative, focused flow. When you start with a photo of something meaningful — a pet, a family snapshot, a child's drawing — the finished piece becomes physical art you can hang on your wall, stick to the fridge, or give as a gift. It's a beautiful way to turn digital memories into something you can hold.

Why BeadSnap is different

1

Real Perler color matching

BeadSnap maps every pixel to the nearest color from the official 38-color Perler palette using perceptual color distance. What you see on screen is what you'll actually build with real beads.

2

Accurate bead count & shopping list

Every pattern comes with an exact color-by-color bead count and a one-click "Copy shopping list" button so you know exactly what to buy before you start.

3

Privacy-first

Your photos never leave your browser. All processing happens locally on your device using the Canvas API. We can't see your images — and we wouldn't want to.

4

100% free, no signup

No accounts, no subscriptions, no paywalls. Just open the page and start creating. BeadSnap is supported by occasional ads and a lot of stubbornness.
