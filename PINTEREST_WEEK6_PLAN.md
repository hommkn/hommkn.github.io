## Pin Image Rules

Use local `pin-generator.html` only. It is intentionally not published on beadsnap.app.

For each pin image:

- Put the main title in the top 25% of the image.
- Do not place important text in the bottom 20%.
- Use before/after images when possible.
- Keep `beadsnap.app` visible but above the Pinterest bottom overlay.
- Use one clear promise per pin.
